Health & Fitness

If you’re having trouble performing a specific movement (e.g. bench press) due to muscle or soft tissue pain, try doing the movement much slower (naturally means less weight too). Some amount of movement is much better than none as it can promote blood flow & reduce atrophy of the affected area. If you’re still feeling pain when performing the movement slowly & controlled then probably should give it more rest and consider seeing a physical therapist or doctor.

Engineering

There’s always a simpler & more elegant solution. Remove as many features of your solution as possible without compromising the core value your solution is supposed to deliver. “If you’re not adding back at least 10% of what you delete you’re not deleting enough” - Elon (paraphrased)

An LLM + RAG with a vector database is the functional equivalent of having an assistant who has memorized the data you loaded into the vector db and is ready to answer any questions about that data and reason about it as you instruct. An important nuance is that the assistant doesn’t have any specialized training. They just have a general understanding of the world & access to your data. To specialize the assistant in a specific task you would need to have them do that task then give them feedback on what they did good and what they did bad & repeat that a bunch of times. That’s what fine-tuning is. If you then give the assistant tools so it can execute actions, it becomes an agent.
