How to be a high output individual and achieve extremely ambitious things

  1. Consistent sleep schedule with at least 7 hours in bed preferably 8+
  2. Exercise regularly
  3. Eat adequate calories, protein, healthy fats, fiber, and fermented foods. Cover micronutrient needs. Moderate toxins including alcohol.
  4. Be a generally happy person
  5. Write down an ambitious goal. Only one.
  1. Figure out the highest impact items for that goal you need to work on that require a singular tunnel of focus and minimal collaboration
  1. Start with 4 hours of deep work a day working on those items & 4 hours a day doing all other tasks that do not require deep focus
  2. Reflect and re-prioritize tasks + re-allocate time as needed
  3. Expect things to take years.
  4. Say no to other opportunities that don’t support this goal. They’re distractions.
